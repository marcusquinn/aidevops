## Quick Reference

- **Font**: NotionInter (modified Inter) — negative letter-spacing at display sizes (-2.125px at 64px)
- **Primary CTA**: Notion Blue `#0075de` — only saturated color in core UI
- **Background**: Pure White `#ffffff` / Alt: Warm White `#f6f5f4`
- **Text**: Near-Black `rgba(0,0,0,0.95)` — not pure black
- **Border**: `1px solid rgba(0,0,0,0.1)` — whisper weight throughout
- **Shadow**: 4-5 layer stacks, individual opacity ≤ 0.05
- **Radius**: 4px buttons/inputs · 12px cards · 9999px pills
- **Spacing**: 8px base unit, organic non-rigid scale
