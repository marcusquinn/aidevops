# Polymarket Times: Depth & Elevation

Prefer broadsheet rules and borders over shadows:

- square cards with 1px black borders
- thick top/bottom rules on cover and chapter banners
- dotted separators for rows/lists where readable
- no rounded cards
- no glossy depth, blur, or neumorphic effects

PDF output should remove decorative shadows and preserve border contrast.
