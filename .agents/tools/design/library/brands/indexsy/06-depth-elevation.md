# Indexsy agency: Depth & Elevation

Use dark glass-like surfaces, large radii, and soft shadows for screen previews. Violet CTAs may use a subtle glow, but report cards must remain readable and source/evidence boundaries must stay explicit. PDF output should remove decorative shadows and preserve border contrast.
