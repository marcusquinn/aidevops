# CHAPTER 6: Direct Response Creative for E-Commerce

Every element either moves the needle or wastes budget.

## Product Photography

**3-Second Rule**: Image must communicate (1) what it is, (2) why it's desirable, (3) who it's for.

**Hero Shot**: 45° front light (soft diffusion), rim light for separation, fill for shadows, 5600K. Product 70-80% of frame, rule of thirds, leading lines. White (#FFFFFF) for feeds; lifestyle for aspiration; blur (f/1.8-2.8) to isolate.

| Category | Primary angles |
|----------|---------------|
| Fashion/Apparel | Front (75%), 3/4 turn, detail, flat lay, on-model (face cropped) |
| Beauty/Cosmetics | 45° open product, swatches on diverse skin tones, before/after, hand for scale |
| Home Goods | In-situ lifestyle (70% of conversions), multiple angles, detail, room-scene |
| Tech/Electronics | Straight-on white BG, 45° showing ports, screen-on demos, size comparison |

| Platform | Ratio | Min Res | Notes |
|----------|-------|---------|-------|
| FB/IG Feed | 1:1 / 4:5 | 1080×1080 | JPG or PNG, sRGB |
| IG Stories/Reels | 9:16 | 1080×1920 | Safe zone: center 1080×1680 |
| TikTok | 9:16 | 1080×1920 | No cross-platform watermarks |
| Pinterest | 2:3 | 1000×1500 | Long-form: 1000×2100 |
| Google Shopping/PMax | 1:1 / 1.91:1 | 800×800 | Pure white BG, no text/logos |

**5-Shot Framework**: (1) Hero — white BG, (2) Lifestyle — aspirational context, (3) Detail — key feature, (4) Scale — size reference, (5) Social proof — 5-star overlay.

**Enhancement**: Consistent color grading, +10-15% saturation, shadow lifting, highlight recovery. Retouching: remove distractions, maintain authenticity, preserve skin texture, seamless BG.

**UGC vs. Studio**: UGC (natural light, smartphone, hands in frame) for cold prospecting, high-consideration, social proof categories. Studio for retargeting, premium/luxury, technical products.

**DCO**: Meta — 10 images/ad set, algorithm tests combos. PMax — 15+ images/asset group (mix formats, 50% text overlays). Test: BG type, angle, composition, model vs. none.

## Carousel Ads

**Card hierarchy**: Card 1 (Hook) — boldest visual, pattern interrupt, social proof. Cards 2-4 (Build) — features, problem/solution, variations. Card 5+ (Close) — urgency, guarantee, CTA, recap.

| Structure | Flow |
|-----------|------|
| Feature Ladder | Each card reveals new feature, building desire |
| PAS | Problem → pain → solution → proof → close |
| Product Range | Variety across segments |
| Social Proof Cascade | Sequential customer evidence |
| Objection Crusher | Address hesitations sequentially |

**Design**: Consistent palette/typography. Progressive disclosure (no redundancy). Swipe incentives (arrows, cliffhangers, numbered cards). Mobile: 40px+ headlines, single focal point, high contrast.

| Platform | Cards | Ratio | Resolution | Notes |
|----------|-------|-------|------------|-------|
| FB/IG | 2-10 | 1:1 | 1080×1080 | 40 char headline, 20 word desc/card |
| LinkedIn | 2-10 | 1:1 / 4:5 | 1080×1080 | PDF upload → image carousel |
| TikTok | 10-35 | 1:1 / 9:16 | 1080×1080 | Auto-advance 1-3s, native music |
| Pinterest | 2-5 | 1:1 / 2:3 | 1000×1000 | Each card links to different URL |

**Advanced**: Catalog carousel (dynamic from product catalog, personalizes by behavior). Multi-product (complementary items for basket building). Tutorial (educational → product as tool).

## Dynamic Product Ads (DPA)

**Events**: ViewContent, AddToCart, InitiateCheckout, Purchase, custom. **Catalog**: Product ID, title, description, price, availability, image/product URLs, category, brand, condition, custom labels.

```text
Product Set: "All Products"
├── "Viewed Not Purchased" (custom audience)
├── "Added to Cart" (custom audience)
├── "High Value Products" (price > $100)
└── "New Arrivals" (date_added < 30 days)
```

**Templates**: Single product (`[IMAGE]/[NAME]/[PRICE] [DISCOUNT]/[STARS] ([REVIEWS])/[CTA]`). Multi-product ("You left these behind" → cards → "Complete your order"). Collection (hero + 4-product grid + "Shop [CATEGORY]").

| Audience | Intent | Approach |
|----------|--------|----------|
| Cart (7d), checkout (3d), viewed 3+ (7d) | Hot | Urgency, cart reminder, discount |
| Viewed product (14d), category (30d), engaged (30d) | Warm | Benefits, social proof, related |
| Homepage (60d), organic, lookalikes | Cool | Discovery, bestsellers, new arrivals |

**Overlays**: Price strikethrough, "Limited Stock", shipping badges, stars, "New"/"Sale" flags. **Text by audience**: Abandoners ("Still thinking about [NAME]? +10% off") | Viewers ("Here's why customers love it...") | Cross-sell ("Also love..."). **Advanced**: Cross-sell (co-purchase), up-sell (premium version), seasonal overlays, location-specific (weather, regional, language).

## Retargeting Sequences

```text
Site Visitors → Product Viewers → Cart Abandoners → Checkout Initiators → Purchasers (Cross-Sell)
```

| Level | Audience | Goal | Timing | Freq Cap |
|-------|----------|------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Site visitors (low) | Brand recall, value prop | 1-30d | 2/wk (10/30d) |
| 2 | Product viewers (med) | Overcome objections | 1-30d | 3/wk (20/30d) |
| 3 | Cart abandoners (high) | Overcome friction, urgency | 1h-3d | 2/day (30/7d) |
| 4 | Checkout initiators (v.high) | Remove final barrier | 1h-2d | 3/day (40/3d) |
| 5 | Purchasers | Repeat, basket expansion | 3-90d | 2/wk |

**Cart abandoner sequence**: Hour 1 ("Still available!") → Hour 4 (social proof: "4.8 stars, 5,200+ customers") → Day 1 ("Free shipping + 10% off") → Day 2 ("Cart expires in 24h") → Day 3 ("Last chance + 15% off").

**Progressive discounts**: None (d1-7) → 10% (d8-14) → 15% + free shipping (d15-21) → 20% final (d22-30). Max by intent: visitors 10% → viewers 15% → cart 20% → checkout 25%. **Rotation** (avoid fatigue): Static → video testimonials → carousel → UGC (weekly).

## Seasonal Creative

| Quarter | Key moments |
|---------|------------|
| Q1 | New Year, Valentine's (Feb 1-14), Presidents' Day, Spring prep |
| Q2 | Easter, Mother's Day (critical), Memorial Day, Father's Day, graduation |
| Q3 | Independence Day, Prime Day, Back-to-school (huge), Labor Day, fall launch |
| Q4 | Halloween, BFCM (peak), Thanksgiving, Holiday shopping, year-end clearance |

**45-Day development window**: Strategy (d-45 to -30: trends, last year analysis, angles) → Production (d-30 to -15: photo, video, design, copy) → Pre-launch (d-15 to -7: account setup, audiences, QA) → Soft launch (d-7 to 0: test small, optimize) → Full launch (d0+: scale winners).

| Season | Palette | Key Angles |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| Valentine's | Red, pink, white, rose gold | Gift Guide, Show Your Love, Self-Love |
| Mother's Day | Pastels, pinks, lavender, gold | She Deserves This, Make Mom's Day |
| Back-to-School | Primary colors, chalkboard | Gear Up, Crush This School Year |
| BFCM | Black, red, gold, high-contrast | Biggest Sale, [X]% Off, Limited Stock |
| Holiday | Red, green, gold, silver, winter whites | Perfect Gift for [Recipient], Last-Minute |

**Holiday phases**: Gift guide (Dec 1-10) → shipping deadlines (Dec 11-18) → last-minute/gift cards (Dec 19-23) → after-Christmas/NYE (Dec 24-31).

**Evergreen balance**: 80% seasonal during peaks, 20% evergreen fallback. Hybrid: `[SEASONAL_OVERLAY] + Product + [EVERGREEN_COPY]`. Swap within 48h of event end.

## Promotional Creative

Every promo ad must instantly show: (1) **The Offer**, (2) **The Value**, (3) **The Deadline**.

| Framework | Format | Best for |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Percentage Off | "30% OFF EVERYTHING" | Sitewide, 30%+ discounts |
| Dollar Amount | "SAVE $50" | High-ticket ($200+) |
| Buy X Get Y | "BUY 2, GET 1 FREE" | Consumables, clearance |
| Threshold | "$20 OFF ORDERS OVER $100" | Higher AOV |
| Tiered | "$100: 15% / $150: 20% / $200: 25%" | Major sales |
| Bundle | "COMPLETE THE SET: $150 (REG. $220)" | Complementary products |

**Visual hierarchy**: Discount (largest) → Product → CTA (contrasting) → Terms (small). **Colors**: Red (urgency) | Orange (flash sales) | Yellow (limited time) | Black/Gold (premium, BFCM) | Blue/Green (trust). **Text**: "30% OFF" not "Get thirty percent off" | "ENDS TONIGHT" not "expires at 11:59 PM PST". **Badges**: Top-right/left, bottom banner, diagonal ribbon — don't obscure product.

**Urgency**: Time-based (countdowns, deadlines). Quantity-based ("ONLY 7 LEFT", "83% CLAIMED", "2,341 SOLD IN 24H").

**15s video formula**: Hook + offer (0-3s) → product montage (4-9s) → urgency (10-12s) → CTA (13-15s).

| Mistake | Wrong | Right |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Confusing terms | "Up to 70% off select items, exclusions apply" | "30% off everything - No code needed" |
| Unclear deadline | "Limited time offer" | "Ends Sunday at midnight" |
| Weak contrast | Light yellow on white | Black on bright yellow |
| Hidden product | Discount overlay covering product | Small badge, product visible |
| Fake urgency | "Last Chance" running 2 weeks | Genuine deadlines, swap immediately |

## AOV-Boosting Creative

### Bundles

```text
"THE COMPLETE [CATEGORY] SET"
Individual (crossed out): $50 + $40 + $35 = $125
Bundle: $89 | SAVE $36 → "Get the Bundle"
```

Types: Complementary (Cleanser + Toner + Moisturizer), Good-Better-Best (Starter/Favorite/Lover), Seasonal (Gift Set).

### Threshold Incentives

- **Free shipping**: Progress bar + suggested add-ons
- **Discount**: "Spend $100, Get 20% Off" + "You're $22 away!"
- **Gift with purchase**: "Spend $75, Get [Product] FREE ($30 value)"

### Upsell & Value Stacking

**Upsell**: Side-by-side Standard vs. Premium with feature checklist. "Most Popular" badge; "78% choose Better" social proof.

```text
YOU GET: Product ($50) + Free Shipping ($15) + Bonus ($20) + Warranty ($30) + 24/7 Support (Priceless)
Total Value: $115 | Your Price: $59 | SAVE $56
```

### Quantity & Testing

- **Volume**: 1×$30 / 2×$27 (10% off) / 3+×$24 (20% off) — "MOST POPULAR" on 3-pack
- **Stock-up**: "Buy 3 = 6-month supply"
- **Gift multiple**: "Keep One, Gift Two. 3 for $99 (Reg. $120)"

**Test variables**: Bundle pricing, thresholds, upsell points, quantity structures. **Metrics**: AOV, units/transaction, take rate, cart abandonment.

### Platform-Specific AOV

- **FB Collection**: Lifestyle hero + 4-product grid → Instant Experience
- **IG Shopping**: Tag multiple products, bundle pricing in caption
- **Google Shopping**: Supplemental feed for bundles, bundle price < sum

## The Direct Response Creative Mindset

1. **Clarity over cleverness** — instant communication or wasted budget
2. **Testing over opinions** — data decides
3. **Speed over perfection** — ship fast, iterate faster
4. **Systems over one-offs** — scalable creative processes
5. **AOV over CPA** — optimize for profit, not just acquisition
